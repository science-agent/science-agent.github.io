Progress, Pitfalls and Paths Forward in AI Agents for Scientific Discovery

A Workshop at NeurIPS 2025

Overview

This workshop will focus on AI agents for scientific discovery, especailly:

Progress

Exploring the current capabilities and achievements of AI agents in scientific domains, from computational reasoning to autonomous research methodologies.

Pitfalls

Examining fundamental limitations, systematic challenges, and critical barriers that constrain the effective deployment of AI agents in scientific discovery.

Path Forward

Identifying emerging paradigms, strategic frameworks, and transformative approaches that will shape the future of AI-driven scientific research.

Call for Papers

We welcome submissions. Topics include, but are not limited to:

Topics of Interest

  • AI agents for experiment planning, execution, scientific reasoning, and hypothesis generation across biology, chemistry, physics, and many other scientific fields
  • Realistic or novel evaluation benchmarks
  • Human-AI collaboration or emerging paradigms
  • Responsible, interpretable, and verifiable design for better Agents
  • Safe non-agentic AI for scientific hypothesis generation and experimental design
